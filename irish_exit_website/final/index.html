Irish Exit is a collaborative show featuring Bethany Hwang, Morgan Nash, Helena Starzec, and Suanna Zhong. After sharing an attic studio space during the summer, the four found a common interest in meditating on moments past. This show focuses on the emptied rooms, vacant scenes, and silhouettes of life. With the intention of drawing the viewerâ€™s eyes to the presence of absence, Irish Exit is in memoriam of times gone by. We are lingering in the doorway before leaving without a proper goodbye.
